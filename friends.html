DINNER WITH FRIENDS

Abby is looking forward to a laid-back Thanksgiving with her best friend, Molly. But, the friends’ plans for a quiet turkey dinner go up in smoke when they’re joined by Molly’s new boyfriend and her flamboyant mother. Throw in some party crashers, including Molly’s old flame, a wannabe shaman, and a trio of Fairy Gay Mothers, and it’s a recipe for a comically chaotic holiday that no one will ever forget—even if they want to!

CAST
