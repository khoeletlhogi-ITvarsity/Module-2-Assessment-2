WITCHES

A young orphaned boy goes to live with his loving grandma in the rural Alabama town of Demopolis in 1967. As the boy and his grandmother encounter some deceptively glamorous but thoroughly diabolical witches, she wisely whisks him away to a seaside resort. Regrettably, they arrive at precisely the same time that the world’s Grand High Witch has gathered her fellow cronies from around the globe—undercover—to carry out her nefarious plans.

CAST

Anne Hathaway, Octavia Spencer, Stanley Tucci, Chris Rock
