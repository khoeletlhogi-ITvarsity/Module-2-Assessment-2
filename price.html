It all began in Durban, South Africa. One idea by two visionaries quickly grew to multiple stores around the country. Fast forward more than thirty years later and now we’re bringing hot fashion at unbelievable value to stores across Africa and worldwide through MRP.com and the Mr Price app! From back in 2012 when our online shopping journey began, we have been working hard to bring you the most convenient way to shop hot fashion. Our Mr Price app offers fashion, homeware and sportswear in one convenient place and has loads of added features like visual search and a super safe, contactless Scan to Pay option. In-store digital receipts save kilometres of paper each year and offer you the convenience of digital access to your shopping history with Mr Price. Choose how and where you’d like to shop by browsing online and finding your wanted item in your nearest store or shopping instore and using the Mr Price app to find an item in your size or taste in another store close to you.
