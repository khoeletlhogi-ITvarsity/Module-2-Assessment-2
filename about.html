PINE CITY MALL

GET TO KNOW PINE CITY MALL

Pine City Mall has just over 120 stores, the mall has a single level with 7 entrances. The mall has a wide walk way, with world-class aesthetics. The design was inspired by malls in the Middle East. Pine city Mall was designed to have a free flow and ability to accommodate a lot of people without the mall feeling over-crowded.

Pine City Mall has three 400 parking bays as well as an under-cover parking which are all free for patrons.

Pine City Mall is easily accessible as it is just off the N14 near Malibongwe Drive
