Following the loss of their son, retired sheriff George Blackledge and his wife, Margare, leave their Montana ranch to rescue their young grandson from the clutches of a dangerous family living off the grid in the Dakotas. When they discover that the Weboys have no intention of letting the child go, George and Margaret are left with no choice but to fight for their family.
