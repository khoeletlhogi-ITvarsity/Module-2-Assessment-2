THE WAR WITH GRANDPA

Sixth-grader Peter is pretty much your average kid—he likes gaming, hanging with his friends, and his beloved pair of Air Jordans. But, when his recently-widowed grandfather, Ed, moves in with Peter’s family, the boy is forced to give up his most prized possession—his bedroom. Unwilling to let such an injustice stand, Peter devises a series of increasingly elaborate pranks to drive out the interloper, but Grandpa Ed wonʼt go without a fight. Soon, the friendly combatants are engaged in an all-out war with side-splitting consequences.

CAST

Robert De Niro, Marisa Tomei, Christopher Walken, Eugene Levy, Juliocesar Chavez
